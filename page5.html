Star Hunting

This might be a spoiler to my Hidden Details page, but star hunting is a new treat available in New Horizons. On random, clear nights after 7PM, Blather's sister, Celeste, will visit your town. The bright red owl will appear in the plaza and wander around your town.

The first time you talk to her she will give you a Star wand. Wands allow you to effortlessly switch between outfits when you're outside of your house. When you have a wand you can go to a closet and pre-select which outfits you can change into. There are a few different types of wands, but all of them need star fragments to make.

Talking to Celeste, she will explain how to get these star fragments. The nights she appears should be meteor showers, meaning shooting stars should appear a lot more often. To wish on a star find an spot on your island that's open to the sky, tilt the right stick up so you're looking up at the sky, and press A when you see a star to wish on it. If you randomly see a star, wait where you are and button mash A for even more wishes! The star fragments will show up along the beach the following day. Special star fragments like cancer fragments and large star fragments.

The rest of the time you talk to Celeste she will give you zodiac and star-themed furniture. These are really stunning so I suggest using them to decorate inside and/or outside your house.
